Introduction

Hi, my name is Krishna Srinivasan. I am currently a senior at James E. Taylor High School(Class of 2026). I coded this website primarily to try and allow AP Physics 1 students to get into the rabbit-hole of learning resources and different perspectives on their topics. I particularly like Walter Lewin’s demonstrations as they actively display some of the hardest conceptual ideas, understanding-wise, in plain life. Moving forward, I will be implementing AI in the website as a free resource to receive test-like questions and get professional answers while remaining understandable. My goal with this website is to provide a free resource for all Physics students to not only discover resources that could help them learn, but also take learning into their own hands, making the process of studying more hands-on and suited to the situation of that student.
